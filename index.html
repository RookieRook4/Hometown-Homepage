Visit Belgrade

Enjoy the culture and beautiful sights in the capital city of Serbia

Places to visit in Belgrade

Belgrade Fortress

A massive historic fortress right where two massive rivers meet. It is the ultimate spot to walk around, explore old stone walls, and catch an amazing sunset view over the water.

Silosi

Old, towering industrial grain silos turned into a trendy cultural zone on the Danube river. It is packed with cool street art, open-air bars, and creative exhibitions.

Ada Ciganlija

A huge lake and park known as Belgrade's "Sea." It has long pebble beaches, cafes, and tons of space for swimming, riding bikes, or playing basketball and other sports.
